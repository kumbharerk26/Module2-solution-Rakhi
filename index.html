Mango

The mango is a member of the drupe family. This is a type of plant food with a fleshy outer section that surrounds a shell, or pit. This pit contains a seed. Olives, dates, and coconuts are also part of this family. There are many different kinds of mango. They vary in color, shape, flavor, and seed size. Although mango skin can be green, red, yellow, or orange, its inner flesh is mostly golden yellow.Mangoes are very famous in india.There are so many varieties of mangoes in present in india with different colors and slightly different tastes.People east both raw and ripe mangoes.They are really loved in india in summer season.

Apple

An apple is a very healthy fruit. There is a popular saying ‘an apple a day keeps the doctor away’. Apples are usually red, yellow or green in colour. They are of different shapes and sizes. They usually grow in hilly regions. Apples are good sources of vitamins. They are useful fruit. Their pulp is used for making juice, jams etc. They are good for health.So many virtues and qualities a single fruit has. An apple is beautiful, tasty and has a fine fragrance too. It can be grown almost everywhere except extreme conditions. It can be kept fresh for several days.
